About Me

Hello! My name is Tammy Lee, and I'm a budding developer, a zealous Product Manager, a lover of the Pacific Northwest, and an amateur farmer. I'm endlessly curious and enjoy asking questions more than answering them.

Product management has taught me to love problems, and programming has taught me to love solutions, so I have the great fortune of seeing both sides of the coin all the time.

When software and its challenges prove to be overwhelming, I enjoy raising chickens, growing lots of vegetables, and creating opportunities for the women of the Western Washington agricultural community to connect and grow together.
